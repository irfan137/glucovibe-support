How do I sync my data across devices?
GlucoVibe uses iCloud to sync your data. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices and have iCloud enabled.
Can I export my glucose data?
Yes! Go to Settings > Data & Sync > Export Data to export your glucose readings, meal logs, and medication records.
How do I set up glucose target ranges?
Navigate to Settings > Glucose Targets to customize your target ranges based on your diabetes type and healthcare provider's recommendations.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We don't have access to your personal health data.
Can I use the app without an Apple ID?
Yes, you can use Demo Mode for local data storage, but you won't be able to sync across devices without signing in.
